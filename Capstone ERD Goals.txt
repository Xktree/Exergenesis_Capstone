Exergenesis App Requirements 

1. Travelers can signup and register 

2. Authenticated users can specify a temperature range 

3. Authenticated users can specify a month they want to vacation 

4. Authenticated users can specify amount of rainfall

5. Can specify country 

6. Can specify continent 

7. Add vacation spots to an array 

8. Delete vacation spots from the stored array 

9. Display all chosen vacation spots on a map with pins
